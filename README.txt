This is a bash script which is a collection of python scripts that will bring you the best(if you don't think so, you can let me know) of the web.
It includes:-
	1. A random wikipedia article
	2. A wikipedia article on a random country
	3. Linux news
	4. Times of India
	5. Economic Times
	6. Slashdot News
	7. Quote of the day from Wikiquote
	8. ...and interesting facts from wikipedia's main page.
	
Upcoming feature...
	1. Updates from your social media like Reddit, Instagram, Twitter etc.
	2. Music video of the day
	3. A daily playlist from Amazon Music. etc...
